About Us

Welcome to Student Tools Hub, the most comprehensive and free resource for students across the globe. Our mission is to simplify academic life by providing fast, reliable, and easy-to-use tools that boost productivity and efficiency.

Founded in 2026, we noticed that students often had to jump between multiple websites to find simple tools like GPA calculators, Pomodoro timers, or Word counters. We decided to build a single, unified platform where every student tool is just one click away.

Why Choose Us?

  • 100% Free: No subscriptions, no hidden fees.
  • Privacy First: We don't store your data. All calculations happen right in your browser.
  • Fast & Responsive: Optimized for mobile and desktop for a seamless experience.
  • No Sign-up: Start using any tool instantly without creating an account.
