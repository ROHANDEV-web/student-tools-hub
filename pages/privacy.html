Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. It is Student Tools Hub's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you across our website.

3. Cookies

We may use cookies to improve your experience. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.
